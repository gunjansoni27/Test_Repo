About Us

Mach Trade Wings

Mach Trade Wings Pvt Ltd, established on 24th July 1995, has emerged as a trusted name in the logistics and trade services industry. It has been a long journey wherein we have honed our skills in freight forwarding & custom clearance. Our mission is to simplify global trade and provide end-to-end solutions that cater to the diverse needs of businesses involved in international commerce. Our extensive range of services is designed to ensure that your goods move seamlessly across borders, whether by air or sea. Our core offerings include comprehensive import and export forwarding services, covering both air and sea freight. We specialize in door-to-door delivery, taking care of every step in the logistics chain—from the point of origin to the final destination.

This includes meticulous coordination and handling, ensuring that your shipments are delivered on time, every time, with the highest levels of efficiency and care. Mach Trade Wings Pvt Ltd is Govt. licensed Custom broker in addition to being a freight forwarder, thus providing comprehensive and fully inhouse import and export services. We can offer advise and provide details of specific requirements for custom clearance in India. We manage all customs formalities and regulatory requirements, navigating the complexities of international trade so our clients don’t have to. Whether it’s preparing and submitting documentation, calculating and paying duties, or securing the necessary approvals, our team is equipped to handle it all. Our expertise extends beyond conventional logistics. We offer specialized services for third-country exports, facilitating complex transactions that involve multiple countries. This service is particularly beneficial for businesses looking to optimize their global supply chain, allowing them to take advantage of international trade agreements and regional market opportunities.

Moreover, we provide a comprehensive suite of DGFT (Directorate General of Foreign Trade) services. Our offerings include all FTA’s (Free trade agreements), advance authorizations, Export Promotion Capital Goods (EPCG) schemes, Remission of Duties and Taxes on Exported Products (RODTEP), IEC procurement, registration of importers & exporters at various ports and various other benefits that help businesses maximize their export potential. We also assist with embassy endorsements, ensuring that all documentation is properly authenticated and meets the necessary legal requirements for international trade. At Mach Trade Wings Pvt Ltd, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We understand the complexities and challenges of global trade, and we are committed to providing solutions that are not only efficient but also cost-effective.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering personalized services that are tailored to meet the specific needs of each client. With a strong focus on reliability, transparency, and innovation, Mach Trade Wings Pvt Ltd is your trusted partner in navigating the ever-evolving world of international trade. We look forward to helping your business grow and succeed on a global scale.
