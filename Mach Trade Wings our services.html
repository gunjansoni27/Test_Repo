Import & Export Custom Clearance

  • Import Custom Clearance

    • Mach Trade Wings Pvt Ltd specializes in import custom clearance for air and sea shipments, providing door-to-door service that simplifies the complexities of international trade. Our team manages all aspects of customs documentation, tariff classification, and duty payment, ensuring compliance with local regulations. We work closely with customs authorities to expedite the clearance process, minimizing delays and ensuring your goods are delivered promptly. Our expertise in handling various types of cargo, including perishable and hazardous goods, makes us a reliable partner for your import needs.

  • Export Custom Clearance

    • Our export custom clearance services by air and sea are designed to ensure smooth and efficient processing of your outbound shipments. Mach Trade Wings Pvt Ltd handles all necessary documentation, tariff assessments, and regulatory compliance, ensuring that your exports meet the required standards of the destination country. We provide door-to-door service, coordinating with carriers and customs authorities to expedite the clearance process. Our experienced team is dedicated to minimizing delays and ensuring that your goods reach their international destinations without any complications.

Import & Export Forwarding

  • Import Forwarding

    • Mach Trade Wings Pvt Ltd offers comprehensive import forwarding services by air and sea, ensuring seamless door-to-door delivery. We manage the entire process, from the moment your goods leave the supplier to their arrival at your doorstep. Our services include cargo handling, documentation, and providing complete visibility throughout the shipment journey. With our extensive global network and expertise in navigating international logistics, we ensure timely and cost-effective delivery, making us your trusted partner for all your import needs.

  • Export Forwarding

Mach Trade Wings Pvt Ltd handles every aspect of your export shipment, from pickup at your location to delivery at the destination, ensuring compliance with international regulations. We offer end-to-end solutions, including packaging, documentation, and coordination with carriers, providing a hassle-free experience. With our focus on efficiency and reliability, we ensure that your goods reach global markets quickly and securely.

Third Country Exports

Mach Trade Wings Pvt Ltd offers specialized third-country export services, facilitating trade between two foreign countries through a third-party location. This service is ideal for businesses looking to leverage international trade agreements and optimize their supply chains. We manage all aspects of the transaction, including documentation, compliance with international trade laws, and coordination with multiple parties. Our expertise in third-country exports allows us to provide tailored solutions that meet the unique needs of our clients, ensuring smooth and efficient cross-border transactions.

DGFT services - All authorizations

Our DGFT services at Mach Trade Wings Pvt Ltd encompass a wide range of authorizations, including Advance Authorizations, Export Promotion Capital Goods (EPCG) schemes, and Remission of Duties and Taxes on Exported Products (RODTEP). We assist businesses in navigating the complex regulatory landscape of international trade, ensuring compliance with government policies while maximizing benefits. Our team handles the entire process, from application to approval, providing expert guidance and support. With our in-depth knowledge of DGFT regulations, we help clients unlock various incentives and streamline their export operations.

Embassy Endorsements

Mach Trade Wings Pvt Ltd offers embassy endorsement services, ensuring that all your export documentation meets the necessary legal requirements for international trade. We assist with the authentication of certificates, invoices, and other critical documents, coordinating with embassies and consulates to obtain the required endorsements. Our services are designed to ensure that your documents are processed accurately and efficiently, reducing the risk of delays in your international transactions. With our expertise in handling embassy endorsements, we provide a seamless experience that ensures compliance with all necessary protocols.
